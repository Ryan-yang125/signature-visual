# Biotech hero brief

The product is a synthetic biology interface. Give the hero a living presence that expresses selective permeability: an external signal crosses a boundary, changes the interior, and the organism repairs. The resting form needs a strong silhouette and an authored mobile crop.

Selected direction: **Permeable Intelligence** — an asymmetric tissue specimen under observation.
