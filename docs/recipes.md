# Worked recipe combinations

Use these examples to understand synthesis. Keep the final visual focused on one thesis.

## Organic intelligence hero

- **Subject:** Living Orb
- **Atmosphere:** a low-contrast Radiance Field
- **Response:** pointer parallax plus a local pulse
- **Annotation:** two or three SVG measurement marks
- **Variation:** project palette, silhouette asymmetry, membrane frequency, text-safe side

## Collaborative agent field

- **Subject:** Particle Current
- **Atmosphere:** ordered grid fading into turbulence
- **Response:** local pointer repulsion and click pulse
- **Annotation:** real workflow states or neutral labels
- **Variation:** field equation, particle archetype, group color mapping, safe-zone mask

## Infrastructure provenance section

- **Subject:** Spatial Lineage
- **Atmosphere:** restrained Meteorological Scan
- **Response:** focus one path on hover or keyboard focus
- **Annotation:** real system stages and source labels
- **Variation:** topology, route geometry, node hierarchy, reveal order

## Climate signal transition

- **Subject:** Resonance Matrix
- **Atmosphere:** Watercolor Drift with data-derived pigment density
- **Response:** scroll moves the system from scattered samples to coherent signal
- **Annotation:** a concise timestamp and data-source label
- **Variation:** sample arrangement, phase map, palette, transition endpoints
